Services

Door Reinforcement

We team up with Night Lock to strengthen door security, safeguarding your home against break-ins and providing a barricade to protect your valuables and loved ones.

Glass Reinforcement

Applying security film to glass greatly enhances its structural integrity, making it much more difficult to break and gain access to the interior of the building or home.

Window Frame Reinforcement

Windows are often considered one of the most vulnerable areas of a home. By applying security film, we not only reinforce the glass themselves but also enhance the strength of the window frames and hardware.
